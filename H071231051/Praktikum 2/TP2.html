About

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a subsidiary of HYBE Corporation, and debuted in July 2022. The group quickly gained attention for their fresh, minimalist style and nostalgic '90s Y2K-inspired music and fashion.

Their music blends various genres, including pop and R&B, and is known for its catchy hooks and refreshing sound that appeals to a wide audience, both in Korea and internationally.

The groupâ€™s name, "NewJeans," is a play on words, symbolizing their aspiration to become a timeless brand in the K-pop industry, just like jeans, which never go out of style.

Their music videos often evoke a dreamy, nostalgic atmosphere, further setting them apart from many of their contemporaries. Fans appreciate their natural charm and relatability, making them one of the fastest-rising K-pop acts of recent years.
