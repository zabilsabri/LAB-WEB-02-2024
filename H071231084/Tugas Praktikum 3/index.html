About Chill Kill

Chill Kill is the latest album from the talented group Red Velvet, reflecting their distinctive blend of catchy melodies and poignant lyrics. This album marks a significant milestone in the group's artistic evolution, showcasing their versatility and depth as musicians. Each song encapsulates a unique narrative, drawing listeners into a world where emotions run deep and every beat resonates with authenticity. With its rich production and captivating vocals, Chill Kill promises to be an unforgettable auditory experience that highlights Red Velvet's growth and creative expression.

The track listing includes standout songs such as Chill Kill, Knock Knock (Who's There?), Underwater, Will I Ever See You Again?, Nightmare, Iced Coffee, One Kiss, Bulldozer, Wings, and Scenery. Each track presents a unique sound and theme, highlighting the individual strengths of the members while maintaining a cohesive vibe throughout the album.
