A Masterpiece by Makoto Shinkai

Weathering With You

Weathering with You (Japanese: 天気の子, Hepburn: Tenki no Ko, lit. 'Child of Weather') is a 2019 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai, produced by CoMix Wave Films and distributed by Toho. It follows a 16-year-old high school boy, Hodaka Morishima, who runs away from his troubled rural home to Tokyo, and later befriends Hina Amano, an orphaned girl who has the ability to control the weather.

About

Weathering with You is directed by Makoto Shinkai, stars Kotaro Daigo and Nana Mori, with animation direction by Atsushi Tamura and character design by Masayoshi Tanaka. The film's orchestral score was composed by Radwimps. Released in Japan on July 19, 2019, it garnered praise for its stunning animation, compelling screenplay, music, and emotional weight. A light novel, also written by Shinkai, was published a day before the film's premiere, while a manga adaptation began serialization on July 25, 2019.

The film grossed US$193.8 million globally, becoming the highest-grossing Japanese film of 2019 and ranking as the seventh highest-grossing anime film of all time. It was Japan’s submission for the Best International Feature Film category at the 92nd Academy Awards. Additionally, Weathering with You earned four Annie Award nominations, including Best Independent Animated Feature, tying with anime classics like Spirited Away and Millennium Actress for the second most nominations for an anime film at the Annies.
